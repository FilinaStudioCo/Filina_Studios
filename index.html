🌸 About the Show

Ano ang Aling Lina?
What is Aling Lina?

A podcast born from passion, friendship, and real stories.

🌺

Our Story

Born from late-night conversations and big dreams, Aling Lina is a podcast by Filina Studios — a passionate 3-person team dedicated to creating authentic, entertaining, and meaningful content.

🎯

Mission / Misyon

To create a space where real stories are told with heart, humor, and honesty — celebrating Filipino culture, experiences, and voices from all walks of life.

✨

What to Expect

From hilarious personal stories to deep meaningful conversations — every episode of Aling Lina feels like sitting down with your favorite tita over coffee.

🎙️ Meet the Hosts

Ang Aming mga Host
The Faces Behind the Mic

Three voices. One mission. Endless stories.

"Busy nga ako but wait — pakinggan mo muna tong idea ko."

Angelina

🎨 The Architect

If you tell her she cannot do it — she will do it. Lahat. Website, logo, music, research, business reports — name it, kaya niya. Kapag sinabi niyang wala na siyang time, that is your sign na may bagong idea siyang paparating — and she will still do it anyway. We do not know how. We stopped asking. 💪✨

"Okay, real talk lang — huwag kayong ano."

Catalina

🎙️ The Heart of the Show

She has been through a lot — and she is still standing. That is exactly why her stories hit different. Minsan hindi mo alam kung iiyak ka o matatawa — and honestly, both is fine. When Catalina shares something, you will feel it in your chest. Yung tipong "hindi ako nag-iisa pala." Yun siya. 🌧️🔥

"It is okay, I am here. Tell me everything."

Rosalinda

📋 The Glue

Walang masamang sasabihin si Rosalinda — kahit deserve mo pa. She is the most patient, the most organized, and honestly the kindest one in this group. She keeps the schedule, the peace, and sometimes our sanity. Kung may gulo, siya ang hahanapin mo. Bes ng bawat isa. 🏆🤍
